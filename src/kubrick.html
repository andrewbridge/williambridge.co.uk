Stanley Kubrick films

They're not light-hearted, but they're definitely memorable. Here's just some of Stanley Kubrick's best films.

2001: A Space Odyssey

Full of memorable, musical scenes, and ever more interesting as AI moves from sci-fi to reality.

A Clockwork Orange

Definitely an 18 and definitely pretty full on to watch (the fact him and his droogs enjoy "a bit of the old ultra-violence" probably gives that away), but A Clockwork Orange has more great music, and bold fashion.

The Shining

A classic horror that keeps you on edge without resorting to jump scares and gore. Chilling music too.
