Drums

This is a bit of a music dump, but here's a selection of music with impressive or interesting drumming!

Tangled Hair - Yeah...It Does Look Like A Spider

Lots of styles going on here, a kind of jazz fusion with some noisey guitars.

The Winstons - Amen Break

The drum break in this track is called the "Amen Break" and it's used in all sorts of other music, but mostly notably drum and bass music.

Buddy Rich

This dude is an incredible drummer, here's just one of his solos.

Steve Gadd

A highly claimed drummer, listen to this, the rhythms are crazy!

Radiohead - Reckoner

I mentioned Radiohead at the top of my music suggestions last year, a great band, and Phil Selway has provided some brilliant drumlines. Reckoner is a favourite of mine.

Muse - Assassin

This track was a favourite among the drummers at school, a great track too!

Jon Theodore

This guy has backed Queens of the Stone Age and Mars Volta and he's just insane.

Whiplash

Finally, Whiplash is a whole film about a drummer. A bit sweary so unsure if your parents would be totally happy with the suggestion, but watch this clip at least!

Happy listening!
